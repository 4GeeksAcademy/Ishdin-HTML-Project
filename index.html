About Me

Hi, I'm Rockstar Romeo, a singer-songwriter based in Austin, Texas. I write folk-inspired indie pop songs about travel, memory, and finding home in unexpected places. Music has been part of my life since I picked up my first guitar at age twelve, and today I get to share that passion with audiences around the country.

When I'm not writing or performing, you can find me exploring small towns, collecting old vinyl records, and drawing inspiration from the people I meet along the way.

Career

  • 2024 — "Roadside Songs" Album Release

    My debut full-length album, written entirely while touring the American Southwest.

  • 2022 — National Tour Support Act

    Opened for indie folk band Willow Creek across a 20-city tour.

  • 2020 — First EP, "Paper Maps"

    A five-song EP that introduced my sound to local audiences and radio stations.
